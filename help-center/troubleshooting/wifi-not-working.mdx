---
title: Wi-Fi Not Working
description: Quick steps to fix Alvelo Redol Café Wi-Fi issues.
---

## Symptoms

- Can't connect to the café's Wi-Fi
- Slow or no internet access

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Wi-Fi Network">
    Make sure you're connected to `AlvesRedolCafe_Free`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept Terms">
    Open any web browser to accept the Wi-Fi terms page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart Device">
    Restart your laptop or phone and reconnect.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Still not working?
Ask a staff member for assistance.
