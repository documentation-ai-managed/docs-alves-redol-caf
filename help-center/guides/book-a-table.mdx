## Reserve Your Table

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Booking Page">
    Go to `https://alvesredolcafe.com/book`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Date & Time">
    Select your preferred date and time slot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details">
    Fill in your name, email, and number of guests.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Reservation">
    Click the "Book Table" button. Check your email for confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
